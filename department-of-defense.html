Meet My Blank

A client had an idea for a new social media site. He had defined the problem the site was intended to solve and had created user personas but could not visualize how the site would work. We were hired to design and develop the new platform.

My role was to collaborate with the lead designer on the project to create user journey maps for each of the user types and create an initial sitemap. This included mapping out each user touchpoint during the onboarding flow and for each of the top tasks. These documents were critical in aligning the entire team’s understanding of the project.
