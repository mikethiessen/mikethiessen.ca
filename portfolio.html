ArtMoi

I was helping ArtMoi define what type of KPIs they should be focusing on to help guide their design and development. I decided to review the experience new members were having when they first signed up. I found that most people were signing up for the free service and then leaving the site without trying to use the software. I found that ArtMoi was forcing first time users to walk through a guide that outlined how to use the software. I thought this might be causing users to abandon the site.

I had a new solution in mind but I wanted to user test it first. We set it up so that 50% of the new users would continue getting the existing experience and the other half would get a new experience. For these users I disabled the forced walkthrough and designed a new landing page that provided quick links to key tasks but did not block them from exploring the site how they wanted. This new method was a success and we quickly rolled it out to all new users.
